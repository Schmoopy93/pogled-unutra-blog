Branches of Psychology

Psychology is a diverse field that can be broadly divided into several branches, including:

  • Cognitive Psychology: This branch focuses on the study of mental processes such as perception, thinking, and problem-solving.
  • Social Psychology: This branch examines how people interact with each other and how social environments can influence behavior.
  • Developmental Psychology: This branch studies how people change and develop throughout their lifespan, including physical, cognitive, and social development.
  • Clinical Psychology: This branch is concerned with the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of mental illness and psychological disorders.
